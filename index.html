Welcome

I'm excited to announce my run for Wisconsin State Assembly. The past 2 weeks I have been out in the community listening to what the people of Wisconsin's 83rd State Assembly District are looking for in a leader. After conversations in the communities of Big Bend, East Troy, Mukwonago, Muskego, Troy Center, Vernon, and Waterford I have decided to enter the race to become our community's next representative in Madison.

As a locally elected Trustee in the Village of Waterford I promise to bring my same no nonsense, hardworking, and collaborative attitude to the Capitol.

I have lived in the 83rd district for over 20 years and was born and raised in Waukesha County. My wife and I have had the privilege of raising four kids in this area and I am so grateful for everything that it has offered. I believe that now it is my time to give back to the community.

As a proud Republican I will make sure to uphold the 83rd District's Republican values. During my time on the Village of Waterford Board I have a proven track record of being fiscally conservative. As a lifelong hunter and outdoorsman, I am a proud supporter of our Second Amendment rights. I will protect the sanctity of life, support our police and first responders, fight for fiscal responsibility, smaller government, and local control. I promise to always be easily accessible to the people of Wisconsin's 83rd and will fight every day that I am in office to better people's lives who live in the 83rd District.

There will be a lot of work to be done between now and our primary on August 9th, but I am so excited to do it. I am humbled by the early support and endorsements that I have received. I look forward to hearing more from the constituents of the 83rd in these upcoming months.
