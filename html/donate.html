Prince is a Vizsla who was born on Februaty 8, 2021. He was born in a litter of 8 puppies made up of 6 males and 2 females. His parents are Gague and Ginger. Prince stayed with his mother and littermates until he was 7 weeks old. He has alwasy been a happy, healthy, curious, and playful puppy.

Some of their favorite exercises include: going for walks, going for runs, swimming, playing fetch, and hunting. They live just about anything that gets them up and moving.
